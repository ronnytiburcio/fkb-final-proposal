5 Contract Terms
Completion & Warranty
  • Project is deemed complete when it can be used for its intended purpose. Minor punch-list items will not delay final payment.
  • All labor is warranted for one (1) year from the date of Substantial Completion. Manufacturer warranties apply separately to all installed products.
Change Orders
  • Any work beyond the original scope requires a written, signed change order before work begins. Change orders may affect the project timeline and total price.
Exclusions
  • Permits, structural modifications, and mold/asbestos remediation are excluded unless explicitly stated in the attached estimate.
  • Unforeseen conditions discovered during construction (e.g., water damage, code violations) will be addressed via change order.
Termination
  • Either party may terminate this agreement via written notice. The Client remains responsible for payment of all work completed and materials procured to date.
Governing Law
  • This agreement is governed by the laws of the state where the project is located. Any disputes shall be resolved through mediation before legal action.
